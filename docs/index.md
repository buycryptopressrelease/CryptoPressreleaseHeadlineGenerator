## Overview

CryptoPressRelease Headline Generator is an AI-powered tool designed to generate compelling, newsworthy, and SEO-friendly headlines for cryptocurrency, blockchain, Web3, NFT, DeFi, and fintech press releases.

It helps startups, crypto projects, PR professionals, and marketers create attention-grabbing titles that follow journalistic best practices while improving visibility across search engines, news platforms, and media outlets.

---

## Features

- Headline Quality Score — evaluates newsworthiness, clarity, and journalistic standards
- SEO Optimization Score — measures keyword density and search engine friendliness
- Crypto Keyword Score — tracks relevant crypto, Web3, DeFi, NFT, and blockchain terms
- Click-Through Potential Score — predicts reader engagement and CTR
- Media Distribution Score — evaluates suitability for news platforms and wire services
